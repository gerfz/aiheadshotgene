Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Portrait Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the limited information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your data.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the absolute minimum data required to provide our AI image generation service:

Key Point: We do NOT collect or store your name, physical address, phone number, or IP address logs.

A. User Generated Content

  • Uploaded Photos: The photos you upload are used solely for the purpose of generating your AI portraits.
  • Generated Images: The AI-generated portraits created by our service.

B. Device Information (Guest Users)

  • If you use the app without an account, we store a randomly generated Guest ID on your device. This allows us to track your free credit usage and display your generated images locally. This ID is not linked to any personal identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information strictly for:

  • Image Generation: Processing your uploaded photo to create the requested AI style.
  • Service Delivery: Displaying your gallery of generated images within the app.
  • Account Management: If you choose to sign up, we associate your generated images with your account so you can access them across devices.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Uploaded Photos: Uploaded source photos are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored in a public gallery.
  • Generated Photos: Your AI-generated portraits are stored securely so you can view and download them from your history. You can delete these at any time within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

To provide our service, we use the following trusted infrastructure providers:

  • Supabase: For secure database hosting and authentication.
  • Replicate: For processing the AI image generation requests.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your generated images directly within the app.
  • If you create an account, you can request account deletion which will remove all associated data.
